Our Journey

How we built VYOM

The milestones, challenges, and breakthroughs that shaped our platform.

YEAR: Q1 2023
VEL: 0 km/s
FUEL: 100%
Q1 2023

The Spark

Satyander and Robins ask: Why do physical classrooms lack real-time insights? We begin initial feasibility and pedagogical research.

Q3 2023

First Prototypes

Built our first real-time attention analysis model using standard webcams and local processing, demonstrating core technical feasibility.

Q1 2024

Patent Filed

Filed our first official patent for real-time classroom student optimization analytics. The core VYOM architecture is finalized.

Q4 2024

Beta Launch

Launched the VYOM beta in 3 pilot schools, tracking attendance and engagement dynamically, generating over 12,000 automated AI insights.

2025 & Beyond

Scale & Impact

Scaling the platform to support hundreds of active classrooms globally, helping teachers create responsive learning environments.

Our Mission

Our Vision
A world where no student is left behind because of data.
