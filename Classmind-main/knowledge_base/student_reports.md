# Student Reports

## What it does
Generates individual diagnostic scorecards detailing a student's accuracy, participation rate, response speed, and coding solutions.

## Why it exists
It provides students and parents with personalized feedback highlighting strengths and specific topics needing study.

## When it should be used
Shared with students after class or during parent-teacher conferences.

## How to use it
1. Go to the student performance roster.
2. Select a student and click 'Generate Report'.
3. Click 'Email Report' to send it directly to their registered email or export it.

## Best practices
Email reports automatically at the end of every week to maintain parent feedback loops.

## Common mistakes
Sending student reports containing blank entries due to student absences during the session.

## Troubleshooting steps
If report emails are bounced, verify SMTP settings and check that the student's email address is formatted correctly.
