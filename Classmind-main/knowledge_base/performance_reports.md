# Performance Reports

## What it does
Compiles class-wide performance metrics, ranking student scores, average response speed, task completion rate, and curriculum success percentages.

## Why it exists
It gives teachers a macro-level view of class achievements and points to learning trends.

## When it should be used
Consulted by school admins and teachers after grading milestones.

## How to use it
1. Open the Reports section.
2. Click on 'Performance Summary' or 'Gradebook'.
3. Filter by session, task difficulty, or date range.

## Best practices
Use the gradebook view to export compiled averages directly into your school's official registry.

## Common mistakes
Failing to check class averages before moving to a new syllabus topic.

## Troubleshooting steps
If data is missing, ensure the tests or tasks have been graded by the system.
