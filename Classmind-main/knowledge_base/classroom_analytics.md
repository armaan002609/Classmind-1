# Classroom Analytics

## What it does
Gathers and visualizes real-time performance analytics including response distributions, time-per-question, accuracy trends, and overall understanding rates.

## Why it exists
It provides instant feedback, helping teachers identify if a topic requires immediate explanation.

## When it should be used
Consulted constantly during active task execution.

## How to use it
1. Open the analytics view on the Teacher Dashboard.
2. Inspect the accuracy gauges and student answering feeds.
3. Toggle charts to view summaries across various task types.

## Best practices
Review the topic confusion metrics during group activities to formulate focused interventions.

## Common mistakes
Assuming low understanding is always an issue; check if the task has a high difficulty level.

## Troubleshooting steps
If data is missing from the charts, ensure the tasks have been sent to and submitted by the students.
