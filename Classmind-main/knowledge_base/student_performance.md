# Student Performance

## What it does
Tracks and benchmarks individual student metrics across tasks, quizzes, coding problems, response speed, and overall accuracy.

## Why it exists
It helps teachers identify specific students who need one-on-one help and recognize top performers.

## When it should be used
Used to monitor student scores and identify struggling individuals during class.

## How to use it
1. Click 'Students' or 'Roster' on the dashboard.
2. Click a student's name to view their performance card.
3. Inspect their task completion rate, average score, compilation history, and raised hands.

## Best practices
Review the performance profiles weekly to track growth trends over multiple sessions.

## Common mistakes
Evaluating students solely on speed; focus on accuracy and completion quality.

## Troubleshooting steps
If a student's performance data seems out of date, verify that their submissions were marked as graded.

## Related features
AI Insights, Analytics, Student Reports
