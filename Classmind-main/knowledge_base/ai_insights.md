# AI Insights

## What it does
Analyzes classroom performance, participation levels, and task answers to automatically identify common mistakes, topic confusion, and students at risk of falling behind.

## Why it exists
It reduces the cognitive load on teachers by transforming raw data into actionable educational advice during and after class.

## When it should be used
Review during class pause points or after ending the session.

## How to use it
1. Open the Teacher Dashboard.
2. Scroll to the AI Insights card or navigate to the Analytics tab.
3. Review the automatically generated summaries of topic confusion and at-risk students.

## Best practices
Use the insights post-session to plan remedial activities or target specific students in the next class.

## Common mistakes
Assuming the insights are absolute; they should be combined with the teacher's personal observation and pedagogy.

## Troubleshooting steps
If insights are empty, ensure students have submitted answers to at least one task or quiz so there is data to analyze.
