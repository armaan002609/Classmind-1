# Classroom Controls

## What it does
Provides master buttons to control the classroom session state, including starting, pausing, resuming, and ending the live class, as well as toggling student chat access.

## Why it exists
It gives teachers complete command over the flow of the class, preventing students from answering questions or chatting when they should be listening.

## When it should be used
Used throughout the live session to transition between teaching modes.

## How to use it
1. Locate the control bar at the top of the Teacher Dashboard.
2. Click 'Pause Session' to freeze student screens.
3. Click 'Toggle Chat' to lock/unlock the global classroom chat.
4. Click 'End Session' when the class has completed.

## Best practices
Pause the session when explaining a complex slide to ensure students' attention is focused on you, not on their task editors.

## Common mistakes
Pausing the session while students are mid-coding, which interrupts their editor typing and could lose progress.

## Troubleshooting steps
If controls do not respond, refresh the teacher dashboard to re-establish the socket connection.
