# Attendance Tracking

## What it does
Automatically monitors and records the entry and exit times of every student joining the session, generating a live presence registry.

## Why it exists
It automates roll-calling, saving valuable instructional time and ensuring accurate participation records.

## When it should be used
Monitored live during class and exported upon session end.

## How to use it
1. Access the Attendance tab on the Teacher Dashboard.
2. View the live table of connected students, their join times, status (Active/Away), and elapsed time.
3. Click 'Export PDF' to download the completed attendance registry.

## Best practices
Cross-reference attendance records with participation percentages to detect disengaged students who joined but did not answer tasks.

## Common mistakes
Closing the teacher server before students leave, which can result in missing leave times.

## Troubleshooting steps
If a student joined but is not in the attendance list, verify they entered the correct 6-digit session code.
