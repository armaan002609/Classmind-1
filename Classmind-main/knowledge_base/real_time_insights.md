# Real-Time Insights

## What it does
Generates instantaneous alerts on teacher screens regarding student confusion zones, task completion speeds, and sudden performance drops.

## Why it exists
It allows teachers to pivot their lecture topics immediately in response to student difficulties.

## When it should be used
Monitored during lecture slides and active tasks.

## How to use it
1. Look at the 'Real-Time Insights' panel on the teacher view.
2. Read notifications like '5 students failed Task 2 within 30 seconds' or 'Confusion detected on recursion'.
3. Take immediate corrective action.

## Best practices
Use these insights to address class-wide errors before moving to independent student work.

## Common mistakes
Addressing errors only at the end of class, after student confusion has compounded.

## Troubleshooting steps
If real-time alerts are not showing, ensure your notification dashboard settings are checked.

## Related features
AI Insights, Classroom Analytics
